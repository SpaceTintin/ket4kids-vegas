ket4kids is a privately operated members-only organization dedicated to assembling small groups of grown-ass adults for the express purpose of birthday joy.

Our Mission

Because Group Texts Are Not a Calendar Invite.

Most group weekends are duct-taped together by a chaotic thread, a forgotten Venmo split, a Google Doc nobody opens, and one stressed friend holding it all together.

ket4kids exists so that doesn't happen. The mission: collect a weekend from good friends and make it count. We handle the logistics. You just show up.

The Mission Is The Weekend.
